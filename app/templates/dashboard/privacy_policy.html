Privacy Policy

update Last Updated: July 10, 2026 • FDA Cleared Class II Software Compliance

Introduction

At CardioViz Analytics ("CardioViz", "we", "us", or "our"), your privacy and the security of your health data are our paramount concerns. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information within our medical analysis unit platform.

By using CardioViz Pro, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the terms of this policy. Our processing of health data is performed in strict compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other relevant international healthcare regulations.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Services, including:

Personal Information

  • check_circle Identification Data: Full name, professional credentials, and medical license numbers.
  • check_circle Contact Details: Professional email address, institution name, and physical office location.

Health & Clinical Data

As a specialized medical diagnostic tool, we process sensitive health information provided by clinicians:

  • ECG waveforms and heart rate variability (HRV) metrics.
  • Patient biometric data (age, sex, BMI) relevant to cardiac risk assessment.
  • Clinical notes and diagnosis history uploaded for analysis.

Usage Data

Automated collection of interaction data to improve diagnostic accuracy and system stability:

  • IP address and device identifiers.
  • System interaction logs and diagnostic query history.
  • Feature utilization metrics.

How We Use Info

We process health data and personal information strictly for clinical diagnostic purposes, quality control, and compliance audits:

  • Analyzing ECG waveform data to deliver accurate arrhythmia risk metrics and visual aids.
  • Enhancing artificial intelligence model thresholds based on de-identified clinical analytics.
  • Monitoring diagnostic query patterns to audit system security and integrity.

Data Sharing

CardioViz does NOT sell or trade your health data with any third-party advertisers. We share information only under these compliant situations:

  • cloud_done Encrypted Cloud Hosting: Handled securely by AWS GovCloud (HIPAA-compliant hosting nodes).
  • science Approved Medical Research: Shared only when explicit consent is provided and datasets are completely de-identified.

Your Rights

Under HIPAA guidelines and international standards (like GDPR), clinicians and patients retain specific rights concerning data records:

  • Right to Access: View and request a complete digital summary of all archived biometric and telemetry data.
  • Right to Erasure: Delete non-regulatory critical history datasets under allowed policy frameworks.

Data Security

We employ military-grade security protocols to protect sensitive health data at all times:

  • Encryption: Standard AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 protocol overlays in transit.
  • Role-Based Controls: Strict Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and RBAC barriers to verify credentials.

Retention

Biometric metrics and diagnosis logs are typically stored for 7 years post-diagnostic, adhering to established medical record compliance laws. Data is automatically archived securely after the policy window expires.

International Transfers

All data is processed within the United States. For international clinics, data transfers comply with standard contractual clauses (SCCs) to maintain the highest levels of safety and privacy standards.
