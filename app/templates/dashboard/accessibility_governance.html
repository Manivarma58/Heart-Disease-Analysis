Governance & Accessibility

At CardioViz, we prioritize clinical precision and universal access. Our data handling and interface standards are designed to support life-saving decisions for every user, regardless of ability.

cookie Cookie Policy

This policy details how CardioViz utilizes cookies and similar tracking technologies to ensure the integrity of analysis data and optimize user performance in medical environments.

check_circle Essential

Strictly necessary for security, authentication, and maintaining HIPAA compliance sessions.

query_stats Performance

Used to monitor system latency and data processing speeds for critical patient monitoring.

tune Functional

Remembers your interface preferences, such as chart density and dark mode settings.

accessibility Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment to WCAG 2.1: CardioViz is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards. We aim for full compliance with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

keyboard Keyboard Navigation

Our analysis dashboard is fully navigable via keyboard, including complex heart disease chart exploration.

record_voice_over Screen Reader Support

Dynamic ARIA labels are updated in real-time as patient data streams into the interface.

contrast Visual Standards

High-contrast color palettes (exceeding 4.5:1 ratio) ensure clarity for clinicians with low vision.

description Data Descriptions

All visual charts provide machine-readable descriptions of heart rate trends and anomalies.

Need Assistance? If you experience any difficulty accessing our software or have feedback regarding accessibility, please contact our Compliance Liaison at compliance@cardioviz.med.
